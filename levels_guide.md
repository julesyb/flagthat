# Flag That - Levels Guide

Flag That has a **10-level progression system** organized into 3 tiers. Levels are purely a measure of mastery - all game modes and content are available from the start. Once you reach a level, you never lose it. Levels are strictly sequential - you must complete each level before progressing to the next.

---

## Tiers

| Tier | Levels | Theme |
|------|--------|-------|
| **Starter** | 1-3 | Core difficulty mastery across easy, medium, and hard |
| **Expert** | 4-8 | Explore and master every game mode |
| **Master** | 9-10 | Prove total breadth and depth |

---

## All 10 Levels

### Starter (Levels 1-3)

| Level | Requirement | How to Get There |
|-------|-------------|------------------|
| 1 | Get 197 correct in Easy mode | Answer 197 questions correctly in Easy difficulty |
| 2 | Get 197 correct in Medium mode | Answer 197 questions correctly in Medium difficulty |
| 3 | Get 197 correct in Hard mode | Answer 197 questions correctly in Hard difficulty |

### Expert (Levels 4-8)

| Level | Requirement | How to Get There |
|-------|-------------|------------------|
| 4 | Get 100 correct in Flash Flag | Answer 100 questions correctly in Flash Flag mode |
| 5 | Get 100 correct in Neighbors | Answer 100 questions correctly in Neighbors mode |
| 6 | Get 100 correct in Flag Puzzle | Answer 100 questions correctly in Flag Puzzle mode |
| 7 | Get 100 correct in Capital Connection | Answer 100 questions correctly in Capital Connection mode |
| 8 | Get 100 correct in Impostor | Answer 100 questions correctly in Impostor mode |

### Master (Levels 9-10)

| Level | Requirement | How to Get There |
|-------|-------------|------------------|
| 9 | Play all 9 game modes | Play at least one game in every available mode |
| 10 | Get every flag right at least 10 times | Correctly identify every single flag in the game at least ten times |

---

## Game Modes Reference

These modes are referenced in level requirements:

- **Easy / Medium / Hard** (Levels 1-3) - Standard flag identification at varying difficulty
- **Flash Flag** (Level 4) - Quick-fire flag recognition
- **Neighbors** (Level 5) - Identify flags of neighboring countries
- **Flag Puzzle** (Level 6) - Puzzle-style flag challenges
- **Capital Connection** (Level 7) - Match flags with their country's capital
- **Impostor** (Level 8) - Spot the fake flag among real ones
- **Time Attack** (needed for Level 9) - Score as many correct answers as possible under time pressure

---

## Tips for Progressing

- **Levels are sequential** - you must complete each one before moving to the next
- **Levels 1-3** require serious commitment to each core difficulty - 197 correct answers matches the total number of flags
- **Levels 4-8** force you to explore every game mode, so don't just stick to one
- **Level 9** requires playing all 9 modes - if you've done levels 1-8, you'll only need Time Attack
- **Level 10** is the ultimate challenge - every flag right at least 10 times takes sustained effort and perfect coverage
