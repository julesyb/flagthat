# Flag That - Levels Guide

Flag That has a **10-level progression system** organized into 3 tiers. Levels are purely a measure of mastery - all game modes and content are available from the start. Once you reach a level, you never lose it.

---

## Tiers

| Tier | Levels | Theme |
|------|--------|-------|
| **Starter** | 1-3 | Mode mastery - prove yourself across all difficulties |
| **Expert** | 4-7 | Depth mastery - get every flag right multiple times |
| **Master** | 8-10 | True mastery - deep repetition across the entire flag set |

---

## All 10 Levels

### Starter (Levels 1-3)

| Level | Requirement | How to Get There |
|-------|-------------|------------------|
| 1 | Get 197 correct in Easy mode | Answer 197 questions correctly playing on Easy difficulty |
| 2 | Get 197 correct in Medium mode | Answer 197 questions correctly playing on Medium difficulty |
| 3 | Get 197 correct in Hard mode | Answer 197 questions correctly playing on Hard difficulty |

### Expert (Levels 4-7)

| Level | Requirement | How to Get There |
|-------|-------------|------------------|
| 4 | Get every flag right at least 2 times | Correctly identify every single flag in the game at least twice |
| 5 | Get every flag right at least 3 times | Correctly identify every single flag in the game at least three times |
| 6 | Get every flag right at least 5 times | Correctly identify every single flag in the game at least five times |
| 7 | Get every flag right at least 7 times | Correctly identify every single flag in the game at least seven times |

### Master (Levels 8-10)

| Level | Requirement | How to Get There |
|-------|-------------|------------------|
| 8 | Get every flag right at least 10 times | Correctly identify every single flag in the game at least ten times |
| 9 | Get every flag right at least 15 times | Correctly identify every single flag in the game at least fifteen times |
| 10 | Get every flag right at least 20 times | Correctly identify every single flag in the game at least twenty times |

---

## Tips for Progressing

- **Start with Easy mode** to build your foundation, then move to Medium and Hard
- **Cover every flag** - levels 4-10 require you to get every single flag right, so focus on flags you haven't seen yet
- **Practice weak flags** - use the Stats screen to find flags you get wrong often and focus on those
- **Consistency is key** - levels 4-10 require getting every flag right multiple times, which takes sustained effort
- **The final level (10)** requires getting every single flag right at least 20 times - true mastery
