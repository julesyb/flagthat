Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 11, 2026

Flag That is a mobile and web quiz game that tests your knowledge of country flags. We believe in keeping things simple - your privacy matters, and we designed the app to respect it from the start.

The short version

Flag That does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All game progress is stored locally on your device. We have no servers, no accounts, and no tracking.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Flag That has no user accounts, no login, no registration, and no server-side data storage.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

Flag That stores the following data locally on your device using your browser's or device's built-in storage (AsyncStorage on mobile, localStorage on web):

  • Game statistics (games played, accuracy, best streak)
  • Per-flag performance (which flags you got right or wrong)
  • Day streak (consecutive days played)

This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to any server, and we cannot access it. You can clear this data at any time from the Stats screen in the app or by clearing your app/browser data.

3. Third-Party Services

Flag That uses the following third-party services to display content:

Flag images - loaded from flagcdn.com, a public CDN for country flag images. When you play the game, your device makes requests to this service to load flag images. These requests may include your IP address as part of standard web traffic. We do not control flagcdn.com's data practices. See their privacy policy for details.

Map tiles - loaded from basemaps.cartocdn.com (CartoDB) when using Map Mode. Similar to flag images, these requests may include your IP address. See CARTO's privacy policy for details.

4. Analytics and Tracking

Flag That does not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services. There are no cookies, no fingerprinting, no ad networks, and no third-party analytics SDKs.

5. Children's Privacy

Flag That is an educational geography game suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Since the app collects no personal data, it complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations.

6. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data. The Share feature in the app generates a text message on your device that you can choose to share through your device's native sharing options. We do not see or store shared content.

7. Data Security

All game data is stored locally on your device using standard platform storage mechanisms. There is no cloud storage, no remote database, and no API. Your data is as secure as your device itself.

8. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it:

  • View your data - visit the Stats screen in the app
  • Delete your data - use the Reset option on the Stats screen, or clear the app's storage in your device settings
  • Export your data - not currently supported, but all data remains on your device

9. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the revised version at this URL and update the effective date. For significant changes, we will also include a notice in the app.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or the app's data practices, please contact us at:

privacy@flagthat.app
